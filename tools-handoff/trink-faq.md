# TRInk FAQ

## What is TRInk?

TRInk is a local Windows annotation overlay for chart markup, trade review, teaching, and video workflows.

## What is TRInk not?

TRInk is not a broker integration, a trading signal product, a trade automation tool, or a platform bypass tool.

## Who is it for?

TRInk is offered as a free tool for registered TradeReality users who want a lightweight local markup overlay.

## Does TRInk require login?

No. Access control happens in the TradeReality app/web download area. The desktop app itself does not require login.

## Does TRInk need internet access?

No network connection is required to start the local overlay, draw annotations, save sessions, or export files.

## Why does Windows show a warning?

Current beta installers are unsigned. Windows SmartScreen warnings are expected until production signing is in place.

## How do I remove it?

Use the normal Windows uninstall flow from Installed Apps / Apps & Features.

## How do I report a problem?

Include the version, what you were doing, any visible error text, and whether the issue involved hotkeys, monitors, sessions, or exports.
